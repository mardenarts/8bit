HJK

Posted on 12 April 2014

Going more alternative, we have decided to make a tribute to the Finnish football! The team that you see here is Helsingin Jalkapalloklubi (HJK), the only Finnish team that made it to the Champions League! Coach: Antti Muurinen Players: Tommi Koivistoinen, Shefki Kuqi, Jarmo Saastamoinen, Hannu Tihinen, Mika Kottila, Markku Kanerva. Bottom row: Piracaia, Aarno Turpeinen, Aki Riihilahti, Ville Nylund, Mika Lehkosuo.
