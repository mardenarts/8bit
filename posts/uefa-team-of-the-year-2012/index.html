UEFA Team of the Year 2012

Posted on 12 March 2013

5.3 million people have voted in UEFA’s website  to choose the team of the year 2012. The selected players were: Iker Casillas (Real Madrid), Sergio Ramos (Real Madrid), Thiago Silva (Paris Saint-Germain), Gérard Piqué (Barcelona), Philipp Lahm (Bayern Munich), Andrés Iniesta (Barcelona), Mesut Özil (Real Madrid), Andrea Pirlo (Juventus), Xavi (Barcelona), Lionel Messi (Barcelona), Cristiano Ronaldo (Real Madrid).
