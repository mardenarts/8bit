Werder Bremen 2003/2004

Posted on 06 March 2013

Werder Bremen team of 2004, who won a double victory in Germany in 2003/2004 season. Thanks to Sebastian from . The six players are: Johan Micoud, Aílton, Ivan Klasnić, Paul Stalteri, Frank Baumann, Valerien Ismaël, Mladen Krstajić. The two trophies you see are called “Meisterschale” and “DFB-Pokal”.
