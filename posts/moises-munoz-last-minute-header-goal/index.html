Moisés Muñoz last minute header goal

Posted on 27 May 2013

Here’s the situation: Mexican League final. Club América was losing 1-0 until 89 minutes. Also, they had lost the first leg 1-0 so they were down 2-0 in minute 89 (there is no away goal rule in the final). In minute 89 América scored a header and in the 3rd min of additional time, the goalkeeper Moisé Muños went up to a corner and miraculously scored equalizer. In addition to that, in the penalty shootouts, then in pks he stopped the 1 shot and after 4 shots América was champion. Thanks to Bernardo Lacaio for the insight!
