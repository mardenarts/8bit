Players who won Champions League and Libertadores

Posted on 26 July 2013

Not that many players get to win the two most important continental cups: Dida (Cruzeiro, AC Milan) , Cafu (São Paulo, AC Milan), Juan Pablo Sorín (River Plate, Juventus), Roque Junior (Palmeiras, AC Milan), Carlos Tevez (Boca Juniors, Manchester United), Walter Samuel (Boca Juniors, Internazionale), Ronaldinho (Atlético-MG, Barcelona).
