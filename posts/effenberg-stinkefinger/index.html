Effenberg Stinkefinger

Posted on 28 March 2013

Dallas, United States, World Cup 1994. After being replaced for Thomas Helmer, Stefan Effenberg hears a ‘booooh’ from German supporters and, in reply, shows his middle finger. As a result, the German coach Berti Vogts decided to drop Effenberg from the rest of the competition. Also in the picture, Lothar Matthäus and Kim Joo-Sung (right?).
