Victor penalty save (video version)

Posted on 02 February 2016

Copa Libertadores 2013, semi final. Atlético-MG are qualifying to the finals with the 1 x 1 result. On the 91st minute, they conceive a penalty. When most thought that was the end, Victor saves the day with a miraculous stop! I hope you all liked the video. Most of the work here was to draw the stadium background. I have been myself to this stadium several times so I wanted to make sure that even the smallest details were present, such as the military police outfit. Special Thanks to Mario Henrique Caixa and Radio Itatiaia from Belo Horizonte for allowing me to use the audio.
