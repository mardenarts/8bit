MLS Cup Final – Sporting Kansas City vs Real Salt Lake

Posted on 07 December 2013

MLS Cup Final 2013! Sporting Kansas City and Real Salt Lake squads. Sporting Kansas City: Jimmy Nielsen, Seth Sinovic, Matt Besler, Aurélien Collin, Chance Myers, Benny Feilhaber, Oriol Rosell, Paulo Nagamura, Graham Zusi, Dom Dwyer, CJ Sapong. Real Salt Lake: Nick Rimando, Chris Wingert, Chris Schuler, Nat Borchers, Tony Beltran; Kyle Beckerman, Luis Gil, Sebastián Velásquez, Javier Morales, Devon Sandoval, Robbie Findley. Thanks to Total MLS for the player selection!
