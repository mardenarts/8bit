Paddy Power’s top 10

Posted on 07 August 2013

Paddy Power has decided to bring the top 10 #BallofShame moments of English Premier League’s last season in 8bits. Here they are: Liam Ridgewell (West Bromwich Albion) wiping his ass with a wad of £20 notes. Aguero’s tackle on David Luiz. David Luiz laughs after this opponent gets a red card. Evra celebrating with a fake arm. Eden Hazard kicking the ball boy. A Newcastle United supporter punches a horse. Balotelli and Mancini fight in the training ground. QPR forced to lock Peter Odemwingie out of Loftus Road after West Brom refuse to give striker permission to speak to club. Luis Suarez bites Ivanovic’s arm. John Terry celebrates the Europa League Title in full kit.
