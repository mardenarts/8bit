Real Madrid 2013

Posted on 01 November 2013

Real Madrid squad of 2013. It was hard to fit 11 players in that illustration, we had to leave some players out such as Isco and Diego Lopez. In the end, after some feedback via Twitter we came up with: Iker Casillas, Álvaro Arbeloa, Sergio Ramos, Raphaël Varane, Marcelo, Sami Khedira, Luka Modrić, Ángel di María, Gareth Bale, Cristiano Ronaldo, Karim Benzema.
