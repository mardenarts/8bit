Marwin Hitz last minute goal

Posted on 22 February 2015

Goalkeeper’s goals are always welcome in 8bit football. In 2013, I made a drawing of Club América’s goalkeeper Moisé Muñoz who scored a header against Cruz Azul. This time, Augsburg’s goalkeeper Marwin Hitz is the featured one. He scored this last minute goal against Bayer Leverkusen in Bundesliga, giving his team a 2-2 draw!
